About Me

I'm an experienced Product and Interaction Designer interested in crafting practical solutions to create meaningful user experiences.

I take charge of the entire design process, starting from research and incorporating innovative interaction patterns to design static and animated interfaces, all the way to production handoff. My goal is to create engaging experiences that can be enjoyed across various platforms, including web, desktop, mobile, and TV.

I’ve thrived in agile product design teams and fast-paced branding and advertising environments. Having deep experience handling high-pressure situations, I have built a reputation for delivering consistent successes based on tight processes and effective communication.

Work Experience

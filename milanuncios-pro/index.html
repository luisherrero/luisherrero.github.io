Milanuncios Pro

A solution to enable users to purchase and activate Milanuncios Pro entirely online.

Addressing key areas such as accessibility, user flow, and product discovery, we designed an end-to-end process that reduces the workload of Customer Support, streamlines the funnel, and unlocks new business opportunities.

Milanuncios offers a professional service that allows business users to manage their listings, access performance analytics, and communicate directly with customers through a dedicated dashboard.

The Challenge

Until now, subscribing to this Pro tool required a phone call with the customer service team. Because of the variety of plans and add-ons, agents also needed to onboard each new customer manually, turning the sign-up process into a major bottleneck for growth.

The main challenge was to simplify a highly complex structure of plans, configurations, and add-ons into an intuitive and scalable self-service flow

Defining the scope

To define the new self-service experience, we first gathered input from every stakeholder: business, marketing, customer support, and legal, to align on goals, technical constraints, and compliance requirements.

The initial scope was quite broad, envisioning several plan configurations and product add-ons that users could combine freely.

The development team was also involved throughout the process, providing technical insights to help us take design decisions.

Validation

Early prototyping tests revealed that too much flexibility created unnecessary friction. After several testing rounds, we needed to simplify the MVP scope to ensure a smoother solution, so in a key workshop with the business team, I presented some strategic approaches that led to the decision to create pre-defined plan packages.

With this approach, we reduced the amount of user's decisions during the process, affecting plan selection and add-ons configuration.

The result was a linear, confident flow with clear steps and messaging, generating a consistent and easy-to-use experience.

Communication is key

Because certain business verticals (Pets, Finance...) had specific rules, we designed a model where all plans are visible online, but those that cannot be contracted digitally generate web-to-lead entries, allowing the customer support team to follow up directly.

We also defined a comprehensive email communication system to ensure a coherent experience across all touchpoints, covering:

  • New subscriptions
  • Cancellations
  • Payment errors
  • Recurring billing
  • Pro tool access

Beyond the flow

To strengthen visual and functional consistency, we customized several third-party components (such as Pay-By-Link services) so users perceived the entire flow as part of the Milanuncios ecosystem.

Finally, I contributed to the motion design of the video tutorials that serve as ongoing support reference for Milanuncios Pro users.

Results

In early November, we launched a simple yet robust end-to-end flow, intuitive for users, but powered by an invisible logic that handles errors and redirects complex cases to the support team via web-to-leads.

Within the first 24 hours, and without any promotional campaign, several online subscriptions were successfully completed, validating both the initiative and the need it addressed.

The new self-service flow now frees up the customer support team to focus on high-value cases, while maintaining full compliance with legal requirements and business needs.

In addition, the entire onboarding process fully meets accessibility standards and reflects the visual language of the Milanuncios design system, ensuring a cohesive, branded, and inclusive experience across the product.

key takeaways

Simplification is a key factor:
Simplifying options for the user was essential. Fewer paths, clearer choices, and a more intuitive flow make the experience faster and more satisfying.

Self-service empowers Customer Support:
The new self-service flow now feeds directly into web-to-leads, making the Customer Support team’s job much easier and helping them focus on precise communication with users.

A purchase solution has many parts:
Building a complete solution goes beyond the checkout — it also means thinking about email flows, third-party integrations, accessibility, and even onboarding videos for new users.

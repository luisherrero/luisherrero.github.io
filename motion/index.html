Motion as a language

As a designer, I firmly believe motion can significantly enhance the visual storytelling of a digital product.

In my career, I have had the opportunity to showcase the strength of this conviction through my involvement in several projects.

Smartly using motion, we can drive the user to a richer, improved experience that is easier to engage with, and therefore we could create an emotional bond between the user and the product.

Media Player Plex

Plex is a SaaS company that provides a streaming service with all media types.
From free, on-demand Movies and Shows to your personal media content on your own server.

A seamless experience

During my time there, I was responsible for the media playback feature, which had to support multiple video and audio formats, like Movies, TV Shows, linear TV, Podcasts, Home videos, etc.

The goal was to have a consistent experience regardless the media type, device, or platform, which included several, like mobile, TV, desktop, or even video consoles.

Get the players

Improving Features

With constant communication with stakeholders, we defined the strategy to improve the experience regardless of the device.

As a result, the features designed and developed have been implemented across all platforms, but they were specifically optimized for each one to ensure a powerful and uniform experience for the customer.

Achievements

I'm really proud of our progress over the years of working on the playback feature, especially all the work related to creating an interface that's simple and intuitive on the surface but has many valuable features for power users under the hood.

Some achievements:

  • Post-play screen & suggestions
  • Ad support (Live TV & On Demand)
  • Chapter support
  • Subtitles offset
  • Lyrics
  • Playback modifiers
  • Sleep timer
